Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pet Analysis. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our application.

2. Data Processing and AI

Our application uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze your pet's behavior. By uploading videos or images, you agree that this media data is processed via Google Gemini AI. We do not use your personal media to train our own models, but it is securely transmitted to Google's API for the sole purpose of providing you with the analysis.

3. Billing and Subscriptions

All in-app purchases and subscriptions are securely handled by RevenueCat and your respective app store (Google Play Store or Apple App Store). We do not store or process your credit card information directly.

4. Data Storage

Your user profile, pet profiles, and analysis history are securely stored using Google Cloud and Firebase. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team.
